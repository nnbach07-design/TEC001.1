THE SUN DIPPED BEHIND THE MOUNTAINS.
A QUIET VILLAGE PREPARED FOR THE NIGHT.
CHILDREN LAUGHED AS LANTERNS FLICKERED TO LIFE.
AN OLD STORYTELLER GATHERED THEM IN THE SQUARE.
HE SPOKE OF A HIDDEN FOREST BEYOND THE RIVER.
LEGENDS SAID A SILVER WOLF GUARDED ITS HEART.
MANY HAD TRIED TO FIND IT, BUT NONE RETURNED.
ONE CURIOUS CHILD, LINA, LISTENED WITH WIDE EYES.
HER HEART BURNED WITH THE DESIRE TO SEE THE WOLF.
THAT NIGHT, SHE PACKED BREAD AND A SMALL LANTERN.
SHE CROSSED THE RIVER UNDER THE PALE MOONLIGHT.
THE FOREST WHISPERED SECRETS AS SHE STEPPED INSIDE.
SHADOWS DANCED, AND THE AIR GREW COLDER.
SUDDENLY, GLOWING EYES APPEARED BETWEEN THE TREES.
THE SILVER WOLF EMERGED, MAJESTIC AND CALM.
IT DID NOT GROWL, BUT BOWED ITS HEAD.
LINA REALIZED THE WOLF WAS NOT A MONSTER.
IT WAS A GUARDIAN, LONELY YET NOBLE.
SHE REACHED OUT, AND THE WOLF LET HER TOUCH ITS FUR.
